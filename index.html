Emerging Fruits & Vegetables Supply Chain

Nestled amidst lush green fields, we are a group of passionate and determined young farmers. We are facing numerous challenges in our agricultural endeavours, from limited market access to a lack of resources and knowledge. However, instead of giving in to despair, we decided to take matters into our own hands and create a solution that would transform our lives and the entire farming community.

One evening, under the shade of a banyan tree, we the farmers gathered together to discuss our shared struggles and aspirations. The idea of forming a farmer producer organisation was born. We envisioned an organisation that would empower farmers, provide us with a collective voice, and enable us to overcome the hurdles we faced.

With unwavering determination, we the farmers embarked on our journey to establish the Kanikho Farmer Producer Company Ltd (FPC). We have mobilised fellow farmers, spreading the word about the benefits and possibilities of joining forces and united strength became a force to be reckoned with.

Together, we the farmers embarked on a journey of growth and transformation. Kanikho FPC will provide access to modern agricultural techniques, training programs, and financial resources. We are here to facilitate access to get the improvised technologies, farm inputs, post harvest management, agri finance and market linkages. The farmers will get increased yields, improved incomes and greater market recognition for their produce.
